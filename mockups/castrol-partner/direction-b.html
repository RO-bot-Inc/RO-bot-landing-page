Pilot Recruiting

What to listen for If a shop owner or manager says any two of these, it's worth a conversation.

Our customers don't trust the recommendations.
The dealership is stealing our customer-pay.
We're terrible at selling video work.
→ Talk about: VIDEO
My techs spend half the day typing.
The writeups on our ROs are garbage.
I can't read what my own techs wrote.
→ Talk about: WRITEUPS
Our MPIs are inconsistent.
Techs are skipping the inspection.
We know there's more we should be finding.
→ Talk about: MPI
I want to be the most modern shop in town.
The kids coming up don't want to write.
I'm losing techs to the dealership.
→ This is a CHAMPION SHOP. Talk about all three.

The 30-second pitch

Say this out loud "It's a voice-first app built for technicians. Techs talk while they work, the app does the writeup, builds the inspection report, and produces a customer-ready video. Shops use it to win more work and free up wasted time."

The three modules Standalone or stacked. Most independents start with one.

W
Writeups
Tech talks, RO.bot writes the 3Cs. Saves 30+ min/tech/day.
  • Voice-to-writeup
  • AI grading
  • Voice edit
  • 3 writing styles
Replaces: hand-typing
M
MPI
Voice-first multi-point inspection. Color-coded, photo-attached, customer-facing report.
  • Voice-driven status
  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Digital customer report

What makes a good pilot shop

5–15 baysBig enough to see real numbers, small enough to move fast.
Key contact identifiedOwner-operator or strong service manager. Tech curious. Will champion the pilot inside the shop and give weekly feedback.
Driving distance from RTPDave can be on-site for setup and check-ins.
