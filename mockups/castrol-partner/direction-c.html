Missed Work

The inspection that never happened.

Tech is rushed. MPI gets half-filled. Findings get missed. The Advisor never gets the chance to recommend the work, let alone sell it.

Declined Work

The recommendation the customer didn't believe.

Tech finds $1,800 of work. Service writer hands the customer a piece of paper with parts codes. Customer thinks they're being upsold. They approve $400.
Cost30–40% of recommended work declined. Thousands per week in walked-away revenue.
PatchAI-guided video walkaround. Tech narrates with a teleprompter. Customer gets a link. They watch. They understand. They approve more.

03
Lost Wrench Time

The valuable minutes a tech spends typing instead of fixing.

5 minutes here, 5 minutes there, between jobs. About 35 minutes per tech across the day. Across 6 techs, ~3.5 hours of lost labor.
CostAt $100/hr, that's $350/day. ~$87K/year. A car or two per day.
PatchTech speaks their notes verbally. RO.bot writes complete, graded, customer-ready writeups. Tech is back on a car in under a minute.
