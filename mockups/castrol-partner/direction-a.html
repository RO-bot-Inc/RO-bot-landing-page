RO.bot for Independent Shops

Turn every technician into your most productive, most profitable tech.

A voice-first AI platform built for the shop floor. Techs talk while they work; RO.bot does the writeup, builds the inspection report, and produces a customer-ready video. Shops use it to win more work and free up wasted time.

What's broken in the shop today

1Customers don't trust the recommendation
Tech finds $1,800 of work; customer hears "they're upselling me" and approves $400. Customer-pay approval sits at 30–40% industry-wide.
2Techs lose an hour+ a day to paperwork
Hand-typed writeups, shorthand, "see attached." Across six techs, that's two cars a day of lost capacity.
3Inspections get rushed or skipped
When MPI is a clipboard or a form to fill out, techs cut corners. Findings get missed. Recommendations never reach the customer.
4Shops don't show their work
No video, no photos, no record of what was actually inspected. Hard to defend a recommendation. Hard to build a reputation.

Value proposition

Trust: seeing is believing; customers approve more work because they don't feel like they are just being upsold.

Throughput: less typing means more wrench time in every bay.

Revenue per RO: more findings discovered, more work recommended, more work approved.

The three modules

Module What it does Key features Best fit
Writeups Tech speaks their notes verbally. RO.bot writes complete, graded, custom-ready write-ups. Voice-to-writeup · AI grading · voice edit · 3 writing styles Shops where techs lose 35+ min/day doing writeups.
MPI Voice-first multi-point inspection. Voice-driven status · photos · videos · digital customer report Shops with low MPI completion or low recommendation approval.
Video AI-guided customer video walkarounds. Tech follows a shot list with on-screen narration. Teleprompter · shot list · customer-shareable link · branded player Shops looking to boost trust and transparency.
