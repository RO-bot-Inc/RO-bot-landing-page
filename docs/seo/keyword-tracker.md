## How to check ranks

1. Open an incognito/private window
2. Search the primary keyword on Google
3. Find the ro-bot.io result (first 10 pages)
4. Record the rank as "N" for page 1 position N, or "11+" if not in top 10
5. Record the date in "Last Checked"

Also worth checking: do Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Google's AI Overviews cite the post when asked a related question? AEO performance matters separately from blue-link SEO.
