# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 1, 2025**  

## Introduction

RO-bot, Inc. ("RO-bot," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of our users, including automotive technicians, service managers, dealership staff, and other authorized users of our AI-powered automotive service documentation platform (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, web application, and related services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information.

By using RO-bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information**
- Name and job title
- Email address
- Phone number
- Dealership or shop name and address
- User credentials (username and password)

**Service Data**
- Voice recordings and transcriptions
- Repair order documentation
- Vehicle identification numbers (VINs)
- Diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs)
- Technical service bulletins (TSBs) accessed
- Parts and warranty information
- Customer vehicle information (make, model, year)

**Communication Data**
- Support requests and correspondence
- Feedback and survey responses
- Training and onboarding communications

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**
- Device type, model, and operating system
- Unique device identifiers
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Mobile network information

**Usage Information**
- Features used and actions taken within the Service
- Date and time of access
- Duration of use sessions
- Search queries within the platform
- Error logs and performance data

**Location Information**
- General geographic location based on IP address
- Precise location (only when you grant permission for specific features)

### Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:
- Your employer (dealership or service center)
- Third-party integrations (dealer management systems, OEM databases)
- Business partners and service providers

### Business Operations
- Create and manage user accounts

### Legal and Safety

- Enforce our terms and policies

### Communications
- Send technical notices and updates
- Respond to your inquiries

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:
