About RO-bot

Revolutionizing automotive service with AI-powered assistance for technicians.

Our Mission

At RO-bot, we're on a mission to transform the automotive service industry by empowering technicians with AI-powered tools that increase efficiency, improve accuracy, and reduce administrative burden.

The Problem We Solve

Automotive technicians spend too much time on paperwork and searching for information, taking valuable time away from what they do best - fixing cars. Our AI co-pilot streamlines these processes, allowing techs to focus on repairs while ensuring comprehensive documentation and improved warranty claim success rates.

Our Solution

RO-bot provides voice-driven documentation, instant access to technical specifications, intelligent diagnostic assistance, and automated warranty claim preparation - all designed to work seamlessly with existing workflows without requiring DMS integration.
